Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 24, 2026  ·  Last Updated: June 24, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how PinGenius ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and handles information when you use our Pinterest affiliate automation application ("the App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

Summary: PinGenius is a tool that automates creating and posting Pinterest pins using Amazon affiliate links. We do not sell your data. We do not store your Pinterest or Amazon credentials on any server. All credentials are entered locally in your browser session only.

1. Information We Collect

PinGenius collects minimal information necessary to operate the App:

  • API Credentials: Your Pinterest Access Token and Amazon Associate Tag are entered directly in the App. These are used solely to communicate with the Pinterest API and Amazon platform on your behalf and are never transmitted to or stored on our servers.
  • Product Preferences: Category selections and pin style preferences you configure within the App. These remain only in your browser session.
  • Pinterest Account Data: When you connect Pinterest, the App may access your board names and IDs solely to post pins to your selected board. We do not store this information after your session ends.
  • Usage Data: We may collect anonymized, aggregated usage statistics (such as how many pins were generated) to improve the App. This data is never linked to your identity.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected solely to:

  • Authenticate with Pinterest's API to create and post pins on your behalf
  • Generate Amazon affiliate links using your Associate Tag
  • Display pin previews and post results within the App interface
  • Improve the performance and features of the App

We do not use your information for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond operating the App as described.

3. Pinterest API Use

PinGenius uses the Pinterest API to create and publish pins to your Pinterest boards. By using this App, you authorize us to take these actions on your behalf using your provided access token. Our use of the Pinterest API is governed by the Pinterest Privacy Policy and Terms of Service. We only request the minimum Pinterest API scopes required: boards:read, boards:write, pins:read, and pins:write.

4. Amazon Associates

PinGenius generates Amazon affiliate links using your Amazon Associate Tag. We are a participant in the Amazon Services LLC Associates Program, an affiliate advertising program. Your Associate Tag and any Amazon PA-API credentials you provide are used only to construct affiliate links and are never stored on our servers.

5. Data Storage & Security

PinGenius operates primarily in your browser. We do not maintain a database of user credentials or personal information. Specifically:

  • Your Pinterest Access Token is held only in your active browser session and is never sent to our servers
  • Your Amazon credentials are used only client-side to build affiliate URLs
  • No cookies are set to track you across sessions or websites
  • We do not use third-party analytics or advertising trackers

6. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell, trade, rent, or share your personal information with third parties except as necessary to operate the App:

  • Pinterest: Pin data is sent directly to Pinterest's API to create your pins
  • Amazon: Affiliate links are constructed using Amazon's standard URL format
  • Anthropic (Claude AI): Product research prompts are sent to the Claude AI API to generate pin content. No personally identifying information is included in these prompts

We do not share your data with advertisers, data brokers, or any other third parties.

7. Your Rights & Choices

You have full control over your data:

  • Revoke Pinterest access at any time via Pinterest Settings → Security → Apps with account access
  • Delete your Amazon PA-API keys from your Amazon Associates account at any time
  • Stop using the App at any time — no account deletion is required since we do not store personal data

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA), United Kingdom, or California, you may have additional rights under GDPR or CCPA. Please contact us to exercise any such rights.

8. Children's Privacy

PinGenius is not directed at children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.
